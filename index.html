Certified Quality and Support

At Patagonia Wood, we are committed to offering high-quality products that not only beautify your home, but also meet the highest standards. Many of our products comply with PDA (Thermal Decontamination Plan) requirements, guaranteeing superior performance.

Our Passion for Wood

In the heart of Temuco, Chile, Patagonia Wood was born, a company dedicated to manufacturing the highest quality wood products. With years of experience, we have perfected the art of working with radiata pine, creating pieces that combine artisanal tradition with the needs of modern design.

Our commitment is simple: to offer durable, aesthetically attractive and functional products that become part of your home.

Mission

To produce and market innovative, high-quality wood solutions for the national and international market, exceeding our customers' expectations.

Vision

To be the reference in the wood products industry, recognized for our excellence, sustainability and ability to create pieces that inspire.
